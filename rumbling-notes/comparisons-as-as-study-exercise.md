# Exercises: Comparisons with "as ... as"

Complete the sentences using the "as ... as" structure with the adjective provided in brackets.

1. This coffee is ___________ (strong) the one I had yesterday.
2. The weather today is ___________ (not/hot) it was last week.
3. My new job is ___________ (not/stressful) my previous one.
4. He speaks English ___________ (fluently) his teacher.
5. This computer is ___________ (expensive) that one.

***

## Answer Key
1. **as strong as**
2. **not as hot as** (or *not so hot as*)
3. **not as stressful as** (or *not so stressful as*)
4. **as fluently as**
5. **as expensive as**

***

## Extra Practice: Transformation Exercises
Rewrite the following sentences using "as ... as" or "not as ... as":

1. The red car is faster than the blue car.
   *(The blue car is ...)*
2. I am a better tennis player than you.
   *(You are ...)*
3. Mathematics is harder than Biology.
   *(Biology is ...)*

*Hint: You will need to reverse the order of the subjects!*
