# Study Exercises: Preferences ("I prefer," "I would rather," "I would prefer")

These exercises will help you practice expressing preferences correctly.

---

## Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks

Choose the correct form based on the structures:
1. I ________ (prefer) watching documentaries over action movies.
2. I ________ (would rather / stay) inside because it's raining.
3. She ________ (would prefer / eat) salad instead of fries.
4. He ________ (prefer / read) books in his free time.

---

## Exercise 2: Identify and Correct

Identify the error in each sentence and correct it.

1. I would rather to go out tonight than stay at home.
2. I prefer tea than coffee.
3. I would prefer go to the cinema rather than the park.

---

## Exercise 3: Rewrite the Sentences

Rewrite the following sentences using the structure in parentheses.

1. I like to walk instead of drive. (Use "I would rather")
2. I like playing tennis more than swimming. (Use "I prefer")
3. I want to drink water tonight. (Use "I would prefer")

---

## Answer Key

### Exercise 1
1. **prefer**
2. **would rather stay**
3. **would prefer to eat**
4. **prefers reading** (or "prefers to read")

### Exercise 2
1. **Error:** "would rather to go". **Correction:** "I would rather **go** out..."
2. **Error:** "tea than coffee". **Correction:** "I prefer tea **to** coffee."
3. **Error:** "prefer go". **Correction:** "I would prefer **to go**..."

### Exercise 3
1. I would rather walk than drive.
2. I prefer playing tennis to swimming.
3. I would prefer to drink water tonight.
