# Exercises: Dependent Prepositions

Complete the sentences by adding the correct preposition (at, in, of, with).

1. I have always been terrible _____ cooking.
2. Are you interested _____ joining our book club?
3. He is very proud _____ his younger brother's success.
4. I am getting really fed up _____ waiting for the bus every morning.
5. She is quite capable _____ handling the project on her own.
6. We were surprised _____ how expensive the tickets were.

***

## Answer Key
1. **at** (terrible at)
2. **in** (interested in)
3. **of** (proud of)
4. **with** (fed up with)
5. **of** (capable of)
6. **at** (surprised at)

***

## Extra Practice
Create your own sentences using the following pairs:

*Tip: Remember to use the -ing form if you follow these with a verb!*
